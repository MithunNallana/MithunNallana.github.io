कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन |

You only have right to do your duties and never to their fruits.

मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि ॥ ४७ ॥

Don't consider yourself to be the cause of results of your work nor to be attached to not fulfulling your duties

Bagavad Gita (Chapter 2, Shlok 47)
